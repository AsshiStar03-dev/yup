Welcome To Rehnumai Foundation

The purpose of Rehnumai Foundation is to help the poor and needy people.
Alhumdullilah! Over the past years, the Rehnumai Foundation has performed its work very well

Our Services

Awarding Scholarship

We provide scholarships to poor children for further studies so that they can make their future successful.

Provide food

We provide food to needy and poor people for survive their life as they want.

Books for Children

We provide books to needy and poor students .we also provide financial assistance to those people in every way.

Provide Medicine

We also provide medicine to people who cannot get treatment due to lack of money.

Installation Water Tank

We make it a water tank in areas where water is scarce and people are deprived of water due to lack of money.

Food For Madaris

For the sake of Allah.we also arrange food for the madrasa children.
